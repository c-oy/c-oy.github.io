About me

我来自温州理工学院 (Wenzhou University of Technology)，目前在机器人工程学院担任讲师（院聘副教授）一职。

I obtained a Ph.D. in Engineering from the Tokyo University of Agriculture and Technology in March 2023.

My research deliverables are mainly in the fields of intelligent robotics (智能机器人) and smart agriculture (智慧农业).

Recently, I developed a novel mobile robot for crop monitoring in greenhouses.

  • ICARM2022

Because there were no similar robots for sale, I made the novel robot by hand. (click ► to play)

It features a monitoring range that can cover all crops in greenhouses.

Using the novel robot, I proposed a tomato disease monitoring system that automatically monitors tomatoes and warns about diseased ones in greenhouses.

  • Agronomy

The animation below illustrates its working process. (click ► to play)

In brief, the system automatically patrols the greenhouse, monitors the tomato plants, detects diseased tomatoes, and reports them to the user.
